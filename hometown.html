History

The scripted history of Hefei can be dated back to more than 3000 thousands years ago when the city of Hefei. In 221 B.C., when the First Emperor of China (Qin Shi-Huang) unified the whole China, Hefei started to be built as a regional cultural and economic center. Experiencing vicissitudes of all the ancient dynasties, Hefei now has become a capital city and the head of Second-Tier Cities in China.

Development and Culture

As the economic and cultural center of Anhui Province, Hefei occupied 11445 kilometer squared spaces which has similar land size of 15 Singapores or 52 Seattles combined! This sounds crazy for just a normal capital city in China. With such large lands, our city holds around one hundred million population. As the center city of Hefei Metropolitan Circle, our development has been pacing forward rapidly with an annual GDP increase rate at 3.5%. In technology field, Hefei is a world-pioneering city for scientific researches, led by University of Science and Technology of China, especially in engineering field.

Since Hefei is a cultural center of the region, local people usually hold many cultural events throughout a year. We hold Lu-Opera Festivals, Three-Kingdoms-Culture Festivals, and even Crayfish Festivals!

Scenic spots

Hefei is a really good sightseeing city with many scenic spots combining history and natural scenarios, but my personal favorite is definitely "Sanhe Town."

Being evaluated as a "National AAAAA level tourist attraction," same as the Palace Museum, Sanhe Town absolutely deserves its reputation. As the origin of Lu Opera, Sanhe Town has well preserved its 2500+ year history in its rivers, bridges, streets and other buildings.

Food

  1. sanhe rice dumplings
  2. braised spicy crayfish

If Sanhe Town hasn't interested you, how about the Sanhe Rice Dumplings? I remember every time I went to Sanhe Town, I could see a huge volume of people standing in the line waiting for rice dumplings. The dumplings are made of rice, white prawns, tofu and usually pork. When you put one of those in your mouth, you could feel the layering texture: the fried crispy dumpling skin is well combined with umami fragrance of shrimp and pork. Not gonna lie, I ate probably 40 of those last time when I visited Sanhe. (I am getting hungry while I am searching the food pictures at this moment = =)

Do you like crayfish? Welcome to Hefei to try for those braised spicy crayfish! If you like spicy food and crayfish, it is definitely the best for you! They are flavorful, appetizing, and SPICY!
